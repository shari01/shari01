## About Me

Bioinformatics Scientist at **Ayass Bioscience, Texas, USA**, with a BS in Bioinformatics (2022) and ongoing MPhil research at **Hazara University, Mansehra**, focused on *Agentic AI-driven biomarker and pathway discovery in Lung Squamous Cell Carcinoma (LUSC)*.

I build end-to-end agentic pipelines that integrate LLM reasoning, PubMed mining, ChEMBL validation, and multi-omics analysis — bridging computational biology with clinical oncology to accelerate drug discovery and precision medicine.

> *"The convergence of agentic AI and multi-omics is unlocking disease mechanisms at a resolution that was unthinkable a decade ago — and we're just getting started."*
